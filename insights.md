# Business Findings

## Revenue Analysis

* Revenue concentration was observed among a limited number of customers.
* Certain regions consistently outperformed others in total sales contribution.

## Customer Analysis

* Repeat customers generated significantly higher revenue.
* Top customers represented a disproportionate share of total revenue.

## Product Analysis

* Technology and Office Supplies categories generated the highest revenue.
* Some categories produced high sales but lower profitability.

## Recommendations

* Prioritize retention strategies for high-value customers.
* Increase investment in top-performing product categories.
* Investigate low-profit categories despite strong sales performance.
